ABOUT ME:
STEPHANIE LEUNG
Hi, I’m Stephanie, a second-year GBDA student. My mood and creativity are shaped by the spaces I create, whether that’s the way I style my room or how I express myself through fashion. With so much of our lives centered around passive scrolling and ai, I’ve learned how important it is to build habits that keep me thinking, making, and engaging creatively.
For me, creativity isn’t just about producing something new — it’s about infusing personal style, perspective, and meaning into everything I do. I’m drawn to projects and spaces that let me experiment, stay curious, and reflect who I am.
I’m drawn to anything that feels intentional: a curated outfit, a cozy corner, a playlist that sets the tone.
